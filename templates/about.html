About Us

Mansoura Robotics is a Student-run organization at Mansoura University
that aims to develop members' hard and soft skills, from Robotics to Data Science,
We have committees that tackle everybody's interests.

Our Vision

Empowering Minds, Cultivating Innovation: Mansoura Robotics envisions a future where students are equipped with a diverse skill set, seamlessly integrating technical expertise and professional acumen. Our vision is to foster a community of forward-thinking individuals who excel in software, hardware, and mechanical design, transcending traditional boundaries to encompass broader areas such as media, HR, business, and event organizing.
By cultivating a culture of continuous learning and collaboration, we aim to inspire the next generation of leaders who drive technological innovation and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

At Mansoura Robotics, our mission is to create a vibrant learning hub where members master software, hardware, and mechanical design, while also delving into non-technical spheres like media, HR, and business. Through hands-on experiences, workshops, and mentorship, we aim to equip our community with diverse skills.
Our commitment extends to hosting the annual 'Hackathon,' a flagship event that educates students beyond our team on the intricacies of our main technical pillars and their sub-categories. Our goal is to inspire lifelong learning, innovation, and leadership among the members of Mansoura Robotics.
